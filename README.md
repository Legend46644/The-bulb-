# The-bulb-
The Bulb
The Bulb is a fast-paced, competitive multiplayer browser game where survival depends on your reflexes, strategy, and ability to outgrow everyone else. Start as a small spark and consume food and other players to dominate the arena.

Built with HTML5 Canvas, JavaScript, and PeerJS, it allows for seamless private rooms so you can destroy your friends in real-time without needing a complex backend server.

Core Gameplay
Consume & Conquer: Eat food pellets to gain mass and grow larger.

Hunt or Be Hunted: Smaller players are fast; larger players are strong. Use your size to trap others, but watch out—the bigger you get, the slower you move.

Smarter Bots: It’s not just humans in the arena. Our AI bots are programmed to hunt you down or flee when they’re outmatched.

Abilities: Spend your hard-earned coins on speed boosts, size increases, and the vital Revive Ability.

Key Features
Multiplayer Servers: Create or join private rooms using unique Peer IDs. No registration required—just share the code and play.

The "Dumb Tip" System: Get hit with high-tier psychological warfare every time you die. Our "Captain Obvious" tips are designed to keep you focused (and slightly annoyed) for the next round.

Ad-Integrated Economy: Short on coins? Watch a quick ad to get back in the action with a revive or top up your bank for new abilities.

Daily Challenges: A rotating cycle of 20 unique challenges to keep the gameplay fresh every single day.

Built for Accessibility: Designed to run smoothly on almost any device, including school or office computers with restricted access.

How to Play
Open the game in any modern web browser.

Enter your name and hit Play for a solo session against bots.

Host or Join a room to play with friends using the Multiplayer menu.

Move using your mouse or touch screen.

Don't die. (Seriously, it's that simple).
